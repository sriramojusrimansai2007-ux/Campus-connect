B.E (Bachelor of Engineering)

A foundational undergraduate engineering degree that prepares students for technical careers across industry and research.

Overview

B.E is a four-year full-time undergraduate program focused on core engineering principles, practical labs, projects and industry exposure. It builds strong fundamentals in mathematics, science and engineering design.

Duration

Typically 4 years (8 semesters). Some universities offer lateral entry (3 years) for diploma holders.

Eligibility

  • 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM).
  • Minimum aggregate normally 45%–60% depending on institution and category.
  • Selection via JEE Main, state entrance tests, or college-specific entrance.

Specializations

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Automotive / Mechatronics
  • Data Science / AI (available at some colleges)

Fees

Approximate annual tuition (varies widely):

  • Government colleges: ₹10,000 — ₹1,50,000 per year
  • Private colleges: ₹60,000 — ₹3,00,000 per year
  • Additional costs: hostel, books, labs, and living expenses.

Admission Process

  1. Register and appear for JEE Main or relevant state entrance.
  2. Participate in centralized counseling or apply directly to colleges.
  3. Complete document verification and fee payment to confirm seat.

Career Scope

B.E graduates can work in software, core engineering, manufacturing, construction, consultancy, R&D, or pursue higher studies (M.Tech, MBA).

Placement Details

Placements range by branch and college reputation. Top roles include Software Engineer, Design Engineer, Site Engineer, Systems Analyst, and R&D positions.

Top recruiters: TCS, Infosys, L&T, Bosch, Siemens, Tata, Mahindra, Microsoft, Google (for top colleges).
