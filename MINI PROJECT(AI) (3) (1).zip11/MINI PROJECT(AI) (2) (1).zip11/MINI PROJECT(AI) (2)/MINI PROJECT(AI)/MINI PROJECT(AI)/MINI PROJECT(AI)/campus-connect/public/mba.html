MBA (Master of Business Administration)

A professional postgraduate degree focused on management, leadership and business skills across industries.

Overview

MBA equips students with managerial knowledge, case studies, internships and practical projects to prepare for leadership roles in business, consulting, finance, marketing and operations.

Duration

Usually 2 years (4 semesters) for full-time programs. One-year executive MBA options are available for experienced professionals.

Eligibility

  • Bachelor’s degree in any discipline with minimum aggregate (usually 50% or as specified).
  • Entrance tests: CAT / MAT / XAT / CMAT / state-level management tests.
  • Group discussion, personal interview and work-experience may be part of selection.

Specializations

  • Finance
  • Marketing
  • Human Resources (HR)
  • Operations & Supply Chain
  • Business Analytics
  • Information Technology Management
  • International Business
  • Entrepreneurship

Fees

  • Government institutes: ₹50,000 — ₹3,00,000 per year
  • Private B-schools: ₹1,00,000 — ₹8,00,000 per year
  • Executive MBA fees are usually higher; scholarships available at select institutes.

Admission Process

  1. Appear for management entrance tests and apply to colleges.
  2. Shortlisting for GD/PI based on scores and profile.
  3. Final selection by merit, interview and work-experience criteria.

Career Scope

MBA graduates enter roles such as Business Analyst, Marketing Manager, Financial Analyst, HR Manager, Operations Manager, Product Manager and Consultants.
